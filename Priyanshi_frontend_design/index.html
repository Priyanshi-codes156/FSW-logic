ABOUT US

We built this at 1:47am, the way most good and slightly unhinged ideas get built. Somewhere along the way, the internet stopped being a place you visit and became a place that visits you: pinging, refreshing, demanding a reaction before you've had time to have one. AfterTwelve0 is the opposite of that. One picture a day. You write what it makes you feel, and that's it. No algorithm deciding if your words are "engaging" enough. No score attached to your soul. We only ask one thing: BE KIND. Not performatively kind. Genuinely, boringly kind. Say something true instead of something clever. If you can't manage that, this probably isn't your place, and we mean that gently but permanently. Everything else, we're figuring out as we go. Just like everyone's best ideas after midnight.

No dopamine, Just feel and write. This isn't a feed. It's a page you visit on purpose.
